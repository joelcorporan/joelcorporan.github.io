SenTinel

A Campus Alert Application

Sentinel is an alert system custom fitted for the University of Puerto Rico at Mayagüez campus public. This mobile application aims to improve the quality of security in campus by providing a quick, reliable channel to communicate with the campus security and the rest of the community while in danger.

Most Effective Ways of Notifications

In reactive ways

In order to alert users in an effective manner, Sentinel implements different forms of notification, allowing seamless interaction with the application. The application allows that the user chooses their notification channel of preference, without requiring to contact the system administrator to choose their notification preferences.

Additionally, Sentinel implements a system to send transmitted mass notifications in case an alert needs to be sent to the community by the administrative staff.

Let Family or Friends know if you have an Incident

They need to know too

Sentinel does not only alert other application users during an incident, but also a select group of contacts you have selected from your family and friends. You are able to add up to five contacts to be notified by text message when you submit an incident.

How Sentinel was Devised

      The commitment with the security of our institution is demonstrated by the creation of tools that improve our quality of coexistence. On August 14, 2014, a professor was stabbed by an offender, leaving him at the brink of death. This fact demonstrated the need of a mechanism by which people can communicate their emergency to the community without delay.

Following this terrible event, the General Student Council and the Administrative Offices sought out solutions. Under the endorsement of these entities, co-founders Joel R. Corporán, Jean C. Méndez and Josué A. Santana developed Sentinel, an application for emergency alerts within the University of Puerto Rico Mayagüez Campus. Sentinel aims to establish an efficient and reliable immediate reaction system that mobilizes officers and the campus community to resolve critical events.

SenTinel is now Available

Sentinel operates at the University of Puerto Rico at Mayagüez and its vicinities. Sentinel is now available in Android and iOS platforms.
